## Public / IP Boundary Clarification

v0.1.3 makes the boundary explicit and consistent:

- This is an open public governance layer and proof standard.
- The methodology, templates, and workflows are available for anyone to adopt or fork.
- Proprietary technology claims, commercial models, sensitive supply chain details, client data, restricted content, or patent strategy do not belong in the public instance.
- Such content belongs in private repositories or redacted releases under appropriate terms.
- Public examples must remain synthetic or clearly public-safe.

This protects IP and operational security while allowing the standard for credible, evidence-based work to spread.

## No Secrets or Sensitive Content

Final package scan found no obvious credentials, keys, internal hostnames, personal data, or restricted information.

Public examples and guidance reinforce the synthetic / public-safe requirement. Any adopting project remains responsible for its own security, compliance, IP, and release review.
